Biography

I am an Associate Reseacher at the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) and State Key Laboratory of Cognitive Intelligence. I obtained my Ph.D. degree advised by Prof. Qi Liu at Anhui Province Key Laboratory of Big Data Analysis and Application .

Research Interests

My primary research interests involve data mining and machine learning algorithms from temporal and sequenced data for applications in life science and healthcare. I am passionate about building powerful (effective and efficient) and trustworthy (privacy-ware and explainable) AI. Secifically, I am interested in the following topics:

  • Time Series Analysis: Specializing in time series representation, including time series classification and forecasting, with a keen focus on analyzing human physiological signals analysis (such as, ECG, EEG, ABP et al) within AI for healthcare.
  • Natural Language Processing: Exploring large language model, including retrieve-augmented generation, in-context learning, and chain-of-thought techniques, to advance scientific literature mining.
  • User Behavior Modeling: Focusing on recommender systems, including sequential/session-based recommendation systems, one-class learning with implicit feedback.
