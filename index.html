Biography

I am an Associate Reseacher at the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) and State Key Laboratory of Cognitive Intelligence. I obtained my Ph.D. degree advised by Prof. Qi Liu at Anhui Province Key Laboratory of Big Data Analysis and Application .

Research Interests

My main research interests include developing data mining and machine learning algorithms from temporal and sequenced data. I am passionate about building precise, efficient, and trustworthy (privacy-ware and explainable) AI. Secifically, I am interested in the following topics:

  • User Behavior Modeling, with emphasis on Sequential Recommendation, Implicit Feedback.
  • Time Series Mining and Prediction with emphasis on Human Physiological Signals in Intelligent Healthcare.
  • Nature Language Understandingwith emphasis on Large Language Model


**欢迎有志于大数据挖掘方向的脚踏实地而又积极主动的本科生、研究生同学加入实验室，与我一同发掘序列数据中的隐藏的知识，也欢迎大家使用我们精心打造的文修智能助写平台进行写作效率与技能提升**
